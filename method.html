HONEY TRAP LIPOSPOME

building a HONEY

A molecular robot, HONEY is a liposome with a receptor of SARS-CoV-2 andmembrane proteins that are needed in the process of infection. This robot hasan amplification system and a fluorescence system inside. To make thisliposome, we use human nasal epithelial cell which has ACE2 receptor,TMPRSS2, and Furin. First, we isolate these proteins by ultrasound. Next, weput magnetic beads that have Anti-ACE2 Antibody, Anti-TMPRSS2 Antibody,and Anti-Furin Antibody to proteins that are isolated. By doing so, we cancollect only membrane fragments which have these proteins selectively.These membrane fragments are used in the process of making liposomes.After that, we use ultrasound to put the amplification system and fluorescencesystem in the liposome. In this phase, membrane fragments are separated forthe moment, but eventually, they are flocculated and forms the membrane again. So we can make liposome which includes the amplification system andfluorescence system inside. This liposome is exactly HONEY. However, there isa 50% chance that membrane proteins will be inside the HONEY.

combination

The system of combination with the virus is as shown below:①Furin, a kind of protease separates the spike protein into S1 and S2.②TMPRSS2, a kind of protease separates S2. Si combines with ACE2③Membrane fusion progresses and the virus enters the host cell.To cause this reaction on the surface of HONEY, we add ACE2 receptor,TMPRSS2, and Furin to the HONEY.

invasionamplificationflorescene

As a way of florescent, we use a chain-substituted system withfluorochrome. We insert the DNA which has the complementary nucleotidesequence in the liposome in advance. In the each of ends of the doublespiral structure of this DNA, we add the fluorochrome and quencher. In thissituation, the distance between fluorochrome and quencher is short. So wecan’t see any florescent. But if there is SARS-CoV-2, the DNA of this virusproduced by the rolling-circle translation reaction replaces one of theoriginal DNA strands. Then, fluorochrome and quencher are separated andcause florescent eventually.
