HONEY TRAP LIPOSPOME

TEAM-A
Honey trap

Our molecular robot is liposome which tells us the existence of SARS-CoV-2 byfluorescent.According to Hoffmann in the journal of Cell in 2020, ACE2 andTMPRSS2 are essential for this coronavirus. Also, on the assumption that thereceptor of this virus is ACE2 and this virus also needs TMPRSS2 and Furin toenter human cells, we add these proteins to our liposome. furthermore, to makeour molecular robot light up in the presence of this virus, we add the systems ofamplification and fluorescence. By fluorescence system, the results of the testwill be determined visually.Next, we explain the differences between HONEY and other testing methods. Aswe said in the part of the background, the target of the PCR is viral RNA. Sothere is a chance that the PCR method reacts to RNA of the uninfectious virus,and it leads to the false positive. As for the antigenic test, it detects theprotein’s binding ability. So this method also has false positives. On the otherhand, our molecular robot, HONEY works as a decoy and it can detect the abilityto infect viruses. So HONEY can improve the false positive and will be a moreaccurate testing method.
